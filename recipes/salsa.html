Salsa Taquera de Chile de Arbol

This Salsa Taquera de Chile de Arbol recipe will literally blow your mind!

Don't expect to have the salsa for very long because once you get a taste, you'll be hooked!

This recipe should take approximately 30 minutes to prepare and blend.

The ingredients are cheap, and the salsa is great on everything!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Tomatillos (no stems)
  • 20 individual Chiles de Arbol (no stems)
  • 2 Chiles Guajillo (no stems or seeds)
  • 1 Chile Serrano (no stem)
  • 1/2 medium Avocado (personal addition)
  • 1/2 Lime (personal addition)
  • 1/4 of White Onion
  • 2 large Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tsp of Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse all ingredients except Chile Guajillo and Chile de Arbol.
  2. Precut Avocado in half; precut Lime in half by removing all outer peel; precut White Onion into one-fourth; peel Garlic;
  3. Remove stems from all chiles, and remove seeds from Chile Guajillo; cut Chile Guajillo in half for four total strips.
  4. Prepare pot with water, and pan with Canola Oil.
  5. Heat water to medium heat until small amounts of water vapor are visible, and heat oil indefinitely on low heat.
  6. Once water vapor is visible, place Tomatillos, Chile Serrano, White Onion, and Garlic Cloves in pot.
  7. Boil ingredients until Tomatillos are a light yellow-green color.
  8. Immediately after Step 6, fry both sides of every Chile Guajillo strip until they darken slightly and curl inward. Move to pot once all are fried.
  9. Immediately after Step 7, fry all Chiles de Arbol simultaneously until they darken and inflate slightly. Move to pot once all are fried.
  10. Turn off heat for frying pan.
  11. When Step 6 is complete, turn off heat for boiling pot, and move pot to an unheated stove-top grate.
  12. Move all ingredients from boiling pot and into blender.
  13. Add Avocado, Salt, and peeled Lime into blender.
  14. Blend to desired consistency.
  15. Taste test and add more Salt or Avocado to preference.
